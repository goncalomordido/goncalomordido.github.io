Biography

Gonçalo Mordido is an incoming postdoctoral fellow at Mila – Quebec Artificial Intelligence Institute, affiliated with Polytechnique Montreal. His current research focuses on efficiently training machine learning models by leveraging algorithm-hardware co-design. Gonçalo obtained his bachelor's and master's degrees from the NOVA University of Lisbon in 2015 and 2017, respectively, and his doctoral degree from the Hasso Plattner Institute in 2021.
