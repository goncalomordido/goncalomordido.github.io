Biography (CV)

I am a postdoctoral fellow at Mila – Quebec AI Institute and Polytechnique Montreal working with Prof. Sarath Chandar and Prof. François Leduc-Primeau. My main research focuses on energy-efficient hardware and algorithms for deep learning.
