Biography

Gonçalo Mordido is concluding his Ph.D. studies at the Hasso Plattner Institute (HPI) and is an incoming postdoctoral fellow at Mila – Quebec Artificial Intelligence Institute, affiliated with Polytechnique Montreal. His current research focuses on efficiently training machine learning models by leveraging algorithm-hardware co-design. During his doctoral studies, he interned at NVIDIA Research twice, having received the NVIDIA Recognition Award for "exceptional and outstanding contributions". Gonçalo obtained his bachelor's and master's degrees in Computer Science Engineering at NOVA University of Lisbon in 2015 and 2017, respectively.
